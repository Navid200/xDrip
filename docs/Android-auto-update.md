# Android Auto Update  
[xDrip](../) >> [Frequently Asked Questions](./FAQ_page.md) >> Android auto update  
  
It is recommended to disable Android Auto Update.  
  
I once had it enabled, and one morning I woke up to find that my phone had automatically updated and restarted. It was waiting for me to enter the password to unlock it—so xDrip wasn’t running.
