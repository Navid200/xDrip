## What to expect and how to reinstall  
[Google Cloud Nightscout](../GoogleCloud.md) >> Major upgrade  
<br/>  
  
There are times you may need to reinstall Google Cloud Nightscout.  
If you delete your existing virtual machine, you will lose your database on it.  And there will be no way to undo the deletion.  

#### **Backup**  
To avoid the possibility of losing your database, you should create a [backup](../).
