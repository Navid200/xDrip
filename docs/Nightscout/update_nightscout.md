## Update Nightscout
[Google Cloud Nightscout](./GoogleCloud.md) >> Update Nightscout  
  
To check your current version of Nightscout, open Nightscout in a browser, click on the hamburger menu button, and scroll down to the "About" section. There, you will find the Nightscout version listed.  
![](./images/Check4Updates.png)  
  
To check if an update is available, visit the “Installation” section of the Google Cloud Nightscout website. The version listed in parentheses indicates the Nightscout version available through a fresh installation or after an update. If your version is older, you can update it using the steps outlined below.  
  
**Note:** You must have already completed the initial Nightscout [installation](./NS_Install.md) steps before attempting an update.  
<br/>  
  
---  

#### **How to update**  
Follow these [steps](./NS_SyncExecutables.md) to update your virtual machine platform to the latest version.  
Once the process is complete, your Nightscout should be updated.  
<br/>  

---  

#### **What if no update is available?**  
After a new Nightscout version is released, there may be a short delay before an update is available.  
During this time, we are verifying the release to ensure compatibility and functionality with our setup. This process may take a few days.  
We recommend being patient while we complete testing and release the updated installation process.  
If you are unsure or need further assistance, feel free to [reach out to us](./GCNS_Support.md).
