## Update Nightscout
[Google Cloud Nightscout](./GoogleCloud.md) >> Update Nightscout  
  
To check your current version of Nightscout, open it in a browser, click the hamburger menu button, and scroll down to the “About” section. You will find the Nightscout version listed there.  
![](./images/Check4Updates.png)  
  
To check if an update is available, visit the “Installation” section of the Google Cloud Nightscout website. The version listed in parentheses indicates the Nightscout version available through a fresh installation or update. If your current version is older, you can follow the steps below to update.  
  
**Note:** You must have already completed the initial Nightscout [installation](./NS_Install.md) steps before attempting an update.  
<br/>  
  
---  

#### **How to update**  
Follow these [steps](./NS_SyncExecutables.md) to update your virtual machine platform to the latest version.  
Once the process is complete, your Nightscout should be updated.  
<br/>  

---  

#### **What if no update is available?**  
After a new Nightscout version is released, there may be a brief delay before the update becomes available.  

We recommend being patient while we complete testing and release the updated installation process. If you are unsure or need further assistance, feel free to [reach out to us](./GCNS_Support.md).
